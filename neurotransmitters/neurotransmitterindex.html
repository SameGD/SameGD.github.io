The Neurotransmitter Project

Fuck neurotransmitters, my brain's gonsta use the blockchain to communicate with itself.

Rationale:

Ok, so my reasoning on this is that there's supposedly a shit ton of different neurotransmitters / neuropeptides / neuromodulators that singal in the brain, but I can only vaguely talk about 10 of them. So, I want to make a page for everyone of these, and write out my research into them. Essentially I want to make a resource that fills the gap between "simplistic website for kids" and "complicated specific research paper" on online resources.

Apparently though, I'm the first person to have ever have even thought of making a full list of the neurotransmitters in the brain, let alone actualy writing what each of them do in the brain. So apparently, completing this project will put my website on the forefront of neurobiological research.
