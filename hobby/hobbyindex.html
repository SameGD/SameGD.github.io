The Hobby Hobby

Whoa they made ADHD into a hobby???

Rationale:

I realised the other day that I have pretty much no hobbies, apart from passively consuming media content and going on long walks. So, I figured that I should probably pick up some more involved hobbies, to build character and all that. Unfortunately, there's like a bazillion different hobbies to pick from, and my brain categorically refuses to make any sort of decsion. So, to spite my brain (or am I enabling it?), I've decided I'm going to make an honest attempt at every trying every hobby on Wikipedia's List of Hobbies, and document my attempts here so I have a greater chance of actually doing this.
