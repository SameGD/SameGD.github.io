Rationale:

Mental Illness information on the internet is complete garbage, it's uninformative, almost never cited, confusing, and sometimes even completely wrong. There needs to be better information on these topics, because they're really important, which is why I'm making this project.

I want this project to cover all the different types of mental illness, giving each of them a short summary, a comprehensive summary of all the different theories as to what causes them, and descriptive information on all the different strategies that are used to treat them. For each of these pages, I want every fact to be properly cited so it's not just me talking out of my ass.

This is a ridiculously large undertaking honestly, but I think that someone needs to do it, and I guess it might as well be me. (Oh shit, I'm essentially giving myself homework, what have I become?)
