Rationale:

I realised the other day that I have pretty much no hobbies, apart from passively consuming media content and going on long walks. So, I figured that I should probably pick up some more involved hobbies, to build character and all that. Unfortunately, there's like a bazillion different hobbies to pick from, and my brain categorically refuses to make any sort of decision. So, to spite my brain (or am I enabling it?), I've decided I'm going to make an honest attempt at every trying every hobby on Wikipedia's List of Hobbies, and document my attempts here so I have a greater chance of actually doing this.

Rules:

I should probably define some rules for what constitutes an attempt, but I can't think of any right now, so that's for future me to figure out. In general, I want to try to do a unique project with each of the hobbies, rather than just attempting them once, snapping a few photos, and calling it a day. I also might avoid some of the hobbies that might actually be dangerous (like BASE Jumping), or somehow re-interpret them so they're not dangerous.
