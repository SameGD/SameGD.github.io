Introduction

As it turns out, I actually enjoy taking care of houseplants. Sure it's not the most involved of hobbies, but there's something really satisfying about checking up on your plants everyday and seeing them grow. Plus giving them names and taking care of them makes them feel like extremely low maintenance pets, which just adds to their appeal.


This page will be dedicated to showing off my steadily growing plant collection, and tracking how my plants change over time.
