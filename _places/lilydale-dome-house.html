The Lilydale Dome House is an abandoned and unfinished house in, you guessed it, Lilydale Victoria. The house is notable for its unique space agey architecture, and for making it's neighbours mad enough to have two separate articles written about how they reallly want it torn down (Oh no, it's taken a small amount of value off their property, better nuke the thing from orbit to be safe). There's not a lot of info about this place besides the neighbours ire, but this page has some great pictures of the place.
